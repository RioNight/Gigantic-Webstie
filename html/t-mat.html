A brief summary on T-MAT:

  • He is the last hero brought into the game and along with HK-206 is one of the only shooter-type robots in Gigantic
  • He shoots precise laser beams, and can store up to three missles to aim at the enemy .
  • He has a jetpack, used to reach long distances or escape enemy encounters.
  • He is very powerful and should not be underestimated.
  • He has limited health, so always watch his health to make sure it doesn't dip down to death.
