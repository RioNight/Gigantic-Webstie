A brief summary on Zandora:

  • Zandora is a playable Hero in Gigantic.
  • She is the Star Knight and is a tank-utility typ player.
  • Also known as ‘The Star Knight’, she is a melee fighter who helps allies with her buffing auras, She was released February 23, 2017 with Eternal Dawn update during closed Beta.
  • She can quickly switch from defense to offense, and from a chase to retreat.
  • When playing Zandora, stick close to allies to get the full worth of her aura buffs.
  • Stay within your team as you fight in order to constantly provide support while being able to strike the enemy frontline with the extended reach on your basic attack.
  • “The Knight moved with the confidence and purpose of a god among mortals. She was the incarnation of valor, a martial blessing in the heat of battle.”
