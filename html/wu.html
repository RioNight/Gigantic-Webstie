A brief summary on Wu:

  • Wu is a highly mobile and effective melee damage hero. He thrives on one versus one combat, and, played correctly, will almost always come out the victor.
  • Manage your stamina – if you don't, you're going to be down and out pretty immediately.
  • Don't depend on [LMB/RT] RAIN OF BLOWS (basic attack) to do your damage, as it's a very low damage attack.
  • Use [RMB/LT] SPLASH KICK to knock enemies in the air and chain attacks. This is also a great push and area negation tactic.
  • [RMB/LT] SPLASH KICKis your best escape skill, so if you at all feel cornered or anything, use it immediately.
  • “At the juncture of brashness and serenity, passion and calm, fury and skill... there is Wu. His training encourages him to leave emotions behind, but he treats every battle as a joyous celebration.”
    — Blau-Grau of the 12 Monks
