A brief summary on Beckett:

  • Beckett is one of the most powerful, and efficent characters in Gigantic, if used correctly.
  • Beckett is a shooter, that can quickly switch from using a machine gun, to using a rocket launcher that can sometimes burn your enemies to smitheriens if upgraded correctly.
  • Because her jetpack boosts her survivability dramatically, turning the most horrific situations upside-down.
  • Beckett also has a gernade that does a lot of damage, and can either crack armor, or poison enemies, depending on what you upgrade it to.
  • Which ever way you choose, know that percision is key.
  • You can use her gernade to pick of escaping enemies.
  • Timing is key to using your jet pack, and you can use it to escape difficult encounters, or to sneak up on your unsuspecting prey.
  • Using her jackpack, correctly you can also go to hard to reach "sniping" or "camping" positions that are used by enemies, and can potentially be used by your team.
  • Together with Charnok, they are an in-seperable duo that can reek havoc through the entire map.
  • Understanding her strengths and weaknesses is key to success, so consider what role you want to be on your team, before picking Beckett.
  • "Fight or flight? I'll take both!"
    — Beckett
