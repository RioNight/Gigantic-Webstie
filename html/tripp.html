A brief summary on Tripp:

  • Playing Tripp is a bit like riding a motorcycle – barely maintaining control, seconds from death.
  • Because her mobility and offensive ratings are high, she is great for attacking from the shadows, and her skill, [E/RB] FLASHDANCE, lets her go invisible for these types of attacks.
  • This comes with the caveat, though, that invisibility is not permanent, and though she might be fast, if she's faced with any significant amount of damage, she's like a paper cup in a typhoon.
  • Consider your attacks, and move with a group.
  • Surviving as Tripp just requires a bit more planning than other heroes, but once you get the rhythm down, she is possibly one of the most powerful and extensible characters on the hero roster.
  • "Tripp is supposed to be the living incarnation of lightning, yet the world knows only her exploits as a thief and assassin. Is she rebelling against her heritage, or is there a subtle plan at work?"
    — The Laughing Cowl, House Melkior
